A Historical analysis of the top 10 companies: trends, patterns and performance

The goal of this project is to identify trends, patterns, and performance variations across different companies over a significant period. This allows for comparisons, potential future predictions, and insights into market dynamics and individual company health. Ultimately, this analysis will enable you to make informed investment decisions and in depth understanding of market behavior.

Summary of Findings:
Apple had the highest volume of traded stocks, while Google had the lowest. 2020 recorded the highest volume of stocks traded, while 2012 recorded the lowest volume of stocks traded.

Recommendation:
Investors should consider diversifying their investments among various companies to mitigate risk and enhance potential returns.
