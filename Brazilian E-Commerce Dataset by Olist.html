Analyzing Olist E-commerce Performance: Insights from a Brazilian dataset

The objective of this analysis is to enhance the performance and customer satisfaction of the e-commerce platform by leveraging data-driven insights to optimize product offerings, improve payment options and streamline order fulfillment processes.

Key Observations from the analysis include:
✪ A total number of 27 states and 609 cities were represented.
✪ Credit cards were the most widely used form of payment by customers.
✪ The total number of customers represented was about 100,000.
✪ Total orders were about 100,000.

Recommendations
✪ Analyzing the broader Brazilian e-commerce market, including growth projections, consumer behavior trends, and digital payment adoption.
✪ This context will help evaluate Olist’s potential for scaling and attracting more sellers.
✪ Monitor Olist’s partnership strategy, especially with large marketplaces and Strategic alliances could drive growth and improve operational efficiencies.
